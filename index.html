About Us Apply Now

United Open Grants is a bold philanthropic initiative that cuts through red tape to deliver real financial help—fast and directly.

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Elena Ramirez, a veteran humanitarian with 25 years of global experience and a PhD from Oxford, we believe generosity shouldn't be complicated.

That's why our End-of-Year Program is open to everyone worldwide: simply request $150–$10,000, and selected recipients receive life-changing grants from $500 to $50,000 on December 31.

No fees. No endless forms. Just transparent, respectful support for those facing challenges or chasing opportunities.

Backed by a small, passionate team committed to integrity and impact, we review every application carefully—protecting your privacy every step of the way.

We're not just giving grants. We're sparking hope and new beginnings, one person at a time.
