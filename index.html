United Open Grants

End-of-Year Grant Support Program – Awards from $500 to $50,000

Welcome to United Open Grants – a global initiative dedicated to providing meaningful financial support during the holiday season.

All individuals worldwide are eligible to apply. Request an amount between $150 and $10,000. Selected grants will range from $500 to $50,000 and be distributed on December 31, 2025.

No application fees. Transparent verification process. Real impact for those in need.
