About Us Apply Now

United Open Grants is a bold philanthropic initiative that cuts through red tape to deliver real financial help—fast and directly.

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Elena Ramirez, a veteran humanitarian with 25 years of global experience and a PhD from Oxford, we believe generosity shouldn't be complicated.

That's why our End-of-Year Program is open to everyone worldwide: simply request $150–$10,000, and selected recipients receive life-changing grants from $500 to $50,000 on December 31.

No fees. No endless forms. Just transparent, respectful support for those facing challenges or chasing opportunities.

Backed by a small, passionate team committed to integrity and impact, we review every application carefully—protecting your privacy every step of the way.

We're not just giving grants. We're sparking hope and new beginnings, one person at a time.

Dr. Elena Ramirez
Founder & Executive Director

With over 25 years of experience in international development and philanthropy, Dr. Elena Ramirez established United Open Grants in 2018 to address gaps in traditional aid systems.

A former senior advisor to global NGOs and recipient of multiple humanitarian awards, Elena holds a PhD in Social Policy from Oxford University. Her career has taken her to more than 40 countries, where she has witnessed firsthand the transformative power of direct financial assistance.

"I founded United Open Grants because I believe dignity and opportunity should not be limited by bureaucracy. Our simple, open application process ensures help reaches those who need it most, when they need it most."

Elena oversees all aspects of the program, ensuring fairness, security, and meaningful outcomes for recipients.
