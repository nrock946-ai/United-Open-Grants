Home About Us

United Open Grants is a private philanthropic initiative founded on the belief that timely financial support can transform lives. Our end-of-year program provides direct, no-strings-attached grants to individuals facing hardship or pursuing meaningful opportunities.

We operate with transparency, integrity, and a deep respect for privacy. Every application is reviewed carefully, and selected grants are distributed efficiently to create real impact.

Dr. Elena Ramirez
Founder & Executive Director

With over 25 years of experience in international development and philanthropy, Dr. Elena Ramirez established United Open Grants in 2018 to address gaps in traditional aid systems.

A former senior advisor to global NGOs and recipient of multiple humanitarian awards, Elena holds a PhD in Social Policy from Oxford University. Her career has taken her to more than 40 countries, where she has witnessed firsthand the transformative power of direct financial assistance.

"I founded United Open Grants because I believe dignity and opportunity should not be limited by bureaucracy. Our simple, open application process ensures help reaches those who need it most, when they need it most."

Elena oversees all aspects of the program, ensuring fairness, security, and meaningful outcomes for recipients.
